This week my research group and I were hosted at the University of Cyprus for an excellent workshop on terrestrial biosphere modelling. The program was really well put-together and it was great to meet my Swiss colleagues in person for the first time.

{% include figure image_path="/assets/images/resized_cyprus.jpeg" alt="Cyprus workshop group photo" caption="Me and the team at UCY" %}

Between our hard work we managed to explore some of Nicosia and sample the local cuisine. The combination of productive research work and experiencing a new environment has given me fresh perspectives on my modeling approaches, and I'm excited to take this forward into my ongoing work.
