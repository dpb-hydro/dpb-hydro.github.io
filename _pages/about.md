I grew up in Nottingham, in the heart of England's East Midlands, before moving to London to study at university. In the final year of my degree, I had the opportunity to spend a year abroad in Hong Kong. After graduating, I returned to Hong Kong to take up my first professional role, arriving just a few months before the world was upended by the COVID-19 pandemic. What followed was a challenging but formative period: five years spent building a career and a life far from home, culminating in the completion of an MPhil degree in 2024.

Since then, I have returned to the UK and now live in Oxfordshire with my wife. I am delighted to have returned to my alma mater, this time as a PhD student. You can find out more about my experience on my [Research](/research/) page, and my [CV](/cv/) sets out my technical background in more detail.
