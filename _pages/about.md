I grew up in Nottingham, a city in the East Midlands region of the UK. I attended university in London, which became my home for three years, and in the final year of my course I had the opportunity to study a year abroad in Hong Kong. After graduation I returned to Hong Kong for my first work position, just a few months before the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic. This was the start of a chanllenging but exciting time away from my home country, a period which lasted five years in total. This culminated with the undertaking of an MPhil degree which I completed in 2024.  

Since completing my MPhil studies I have returned to the UK, where I now live in Oxfordshire with my wife. From here it's a straighforward commute to London, where I am very happy to have returned to my alma mater to study a PhD. You can find out about my project on my [Research](/research/) page, and see my [CV](/cv/) for details of my technical skills.
