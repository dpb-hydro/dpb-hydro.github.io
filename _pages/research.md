I am a PhD student at Imperial College London specialising in numerical modelling of the hydrology of land surface ecosystems. I work to develop simulations that help us better understand how natural systems respond to various conditions and changes. I have an MEng degree in civil engineering and an MPhil degree in marine environmental science (physical oceanography), through which I have a foundation in engineering mathematics and dynamical systems modelling and analysis. Through my work I am competent in the use of four computing languages and of high-performance computers. See my [CV](/cv/) for more details.
